Hi, I'm Ashley! I'm a designer who loves doing “everything, everywhere, all at once” — engaging with a multiplicity of disciplines and connecting the dots between them to arrive at solutions. I thrive at the intersection of design, strategy, and marketing for products, services, systems, and experiences

I'm a multidisciplinary designer based in the SF Bay Area, currently working to promote awareness of cyber threats and security best practices at Adobe. I completed my Bachelor of Design at the University of Alberta in Canada in 2023, majoring in visual communication and product design with a specialization in business and marketing.
